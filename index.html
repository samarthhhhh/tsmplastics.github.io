About Us

TSM Plastics, located at D-20, Sector-7, Noida (U.P.), has over 26 years of experience in plastic manufacturing. We specialize in injection molding, blow molding, and PET stretch molding for FMCG and export-oriented clients. With over 24 machines and an in-house tool room, we deliver high-quality, innovative components across industries.

Our motto: Work closely with customers and deliver precision products with continuous innovation. Our services span caps, handles, trays, containers, cosmetic packaging, automotive parts, and more. Some product details remain confidential due to client agreements.
