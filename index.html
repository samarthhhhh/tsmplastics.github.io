About Us

TSM Plastics has been shaping the future of plastic manufacturing for over 30 years. We specialize in high-precision injection molding, blow molding, and PET stretch blow molding— delivering custom solutions to leaders across FMCG, automotive, medical, packaging, and engineering sectors.

Our state-of-the-art facility has consistently delivered high-quality, cost-effective, and industry-specific plastic solutions to a global clientele.

Whether you’re a growing brand or an established name, we help you scale with reliable, innovative plastic solutions.
